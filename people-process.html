About the Speakers

Matt MacInnis Matt MacInnis is the Founder and CEO of Inkling. With a vision for reinventing how knowledge is shared, Matt is passionate about making Inkling the leading platform for how content is built, distributed and consumed. Prior to founding Inkling in 2009, Matt managed Apple’s expansion into international education markets.

Maureen McMahon Maureen McMahon is president and publisher of Kaplan Publishing, one of the nation’s leading publishers of academic and professional resources in print and digital formats. In addition to overseeing content production, delivery, and management for Kaplan Test Prep course and retail products, she develops initiatives that focus on leveraging emerging technologies to improve learning outcomes.
